## How It Works

**Before the meeting:**
- Post a tip, question, or topic idea in the [Discussion Doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1nWpwEteZ0sx115RrWjOYY9kPmZorbkwVXmZ-b6MYYr8/edit?usp=sharing)
- The week's presenter prepares a short demo or walkthrough

**During the meeting (50 min):**
1. **Demo / Presentation** (~20 min) - The presenter shares a Claude workflow, tip, or project
2. **Open Discussion** (~20 min) - Q&A, troubleshooting, sharing related experiences
3. **Lightning Tips** (~10 min) - Anyone can share a quick tip or trick

**After the meeting:**
- Meeting notes are added to this site under [Notes](/notes/)
- Continue the conversation in the [Discussion Doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1nWpwEteZ0sx115RrWjOYY9kPmZorbkwVXmZ-b6MYYr8/edit?usp=sharing)
